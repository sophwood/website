About Me







I was born and raised on the west side of Cleveland, Ohio. I attended Magnificat High School, where my favorite activities were playing on the tennis team and competing in Lincoln Douglas debate.


After graduating from Magnificat in 2013, I began attending The University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I began Penn as a major in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering. However, by the fall of my Sophomore year, I realized I wanted to change my major to Criminology, as it better suited my interests in the studies of law and behavioral psychology.


Once I had officially changed my major, I decided to also minor in Computer Science. My interest in CS was piqued by exposure to programs created by my classmates. I decided I too wanted to be able to write code to, well, "do stuff."


I'm graduating from Penn on May 15th, 2017. Afterward, I plan to spend some time at home in Ohio with my parents, Tom and Marina, as well as my brother, Daniel. In early July, I will move to San Francisco to pursue a yet-to-be-decided career which I hope will allow me to develop my interests in data analysis and behavioral science. Stay tuned for updates!
